Anton Korenyako

X5 Retail Group Karusel Website

Karusel is one of the largest hypermarket chains in Russia, managed by X5 Retail Group, a leading Russian food retailer. I was a part of team working on the Karusel online store.

My Role

Working closely with X5 Retail Marketing Team for over 5 months, my role was to design new online store from scratch for both web and mobile. This included working on visual design, user experience and rapid prototyping.

What I did

  • Ideation
  • User Flows
  • Wireframing
  • Prototyping
  • Mockups
