Portfolio / X5 Group Karusel Website

Karusel is a large chain of hypermarkets (56 stores), managed by X5 Group, a leading Russian food retailer. I was a part of team working on the Karusel online store.

My Role

Working closely with X5 Retail Marketing Team for over 5 months, my role was to design new online store from scratch for both web and mobile. This included working on visual design, user experience and rapid prototyping.

What I did

  • Ideation
  • User Flows
  • Wireframing
  • Prototyping
  • Mockups

I scoped out Karusel competitors and other X5 websites and made observations on which layouts worked best. I sketched out possible solutions on paper for the main screens before committing to high fidelity mockups.

Given the accelerated lead time for this project, we couldn't afford to start the project with wireframes.

Also using wireframes before for presenting journeys to the X5 team had not been successful. Stakeholders found it difficult to visualise how grey rectangles could transform into the final website.




Therefore, I immediately began to make high fidelity mockups. I am proud that I met the expectations of the client on the first try. In addition to the mobile app, I refreshed their visual style and designed a reusable component library.
