Anton Korenyako

Portfolio / Digital Illustrations

For many digital projects I have done graphics by myself. I use Xara, renowned for it's ultra fast vector rendering engine.

For Google Russia I created a series of banners with slogan 'They are in search of you' (Они ищут вас). I used popular search queries to show how Google Ads platform can be used to attract customers.

The result was a Halloween advertising campaign with mummies searching hot tours to Egypt, zombies looking for bloody steak recipes and ghosts in search of attic rentals.

In 2017 I set up the Xara users community on Behance and my works were featured in official Xara publication, Xara Xone: http://www.xaraxone.com/us/art/anton_korenyako/
